Our History

We are part of "Elitser Group" established in 1999. The company started its middle east operations in 2003. Formerly it was recognized as Primajava Softech Fz LLC. The company has a registered office in Dubai's Techno hub Barsha Heights. Apart from Dubai, the company has offices in Abu Dhabi, Bahrain, India, Singapore, Bangladesh and Indonesia.
