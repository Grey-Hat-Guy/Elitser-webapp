Hardware Asset Management

Our Hardware Asset Management services cover end-to-end tracking and control of your physical IT assets. This includes tagging each asset using barcodes, RFID, or E-tags to ensure accurate identification. We help organizations maintain real-time inventory tracking and reconciliation, preventing asset misplacement or duplication. Regular physical audits are conducted to verify asset presence, condition, and location. In addition, we offer lifecycle reporting that provides insights into the acquisition, utilization, depreciation, and end-of-life of assets, enabling better decision-making and financial planning.

Software Asset Management

We provide robust Software Asset Management solutions to help you control software usage and costs effectively. Our services include optimizing license allocation by identifying underutilized or unused software, thus eliminating unnecessary expenses. Real-time usage monitoring helps you assess actual demand and improve license efficiency. We ensure audit preparedness by maintaining accurate records of software entitlements, installations, and usage, ensuring compliance with vendor terms. Configuration Management

Our Configuration Management service focuses on establishing and maintaining an accurate and dynamic Configuration Management Database (CMDB). We start by identifying and classifying all Configuration Items (CIs), including hardware, software, and network components. We map relationships between these CIs to help understand service dependencies and improve root cause analysis during incidents. By tracking version changes and updates, we support better change control and risk management. Governance is maintained through strict access controls and audit trails, ensuring data confidentiality and integrity throughout the system lifecycle.

Elitser's Contract and Warranty Tracking solutions offer centralized management of all IT-related agreements including software licenses, SLAs, AMCs, and vendor contracts.
