Asset Tagging Services

Asset tagging is the foundation of effective asset management. We provide professional asset tagging services to uniquely identify and track every IT and OT asset.
Our Asset Tagging Capabilities Include:

  • Barcode, QR code, and RFID tagging
  • Durable tags suitable for industrial and harsh environments
  • Asset labeling based on organizational standards
  • Integration with asset management systems
  • Physical verification and reconciliation
Benefits of Asset Tagging:
  • Eliminates asset loss and misidentification
  • Enables faster audits and inventory checks
  • Improves accountability and ownership
  • Enhances lifecycle and warranty tracking

Configuration Management

Our Configuration Management service focuses on establishing and maintaining an accurate and dynamic Configuration Management Database (CMDB). We start by identifying and classifying all Configuration Items (CIs), including hardware, software, and network components. We map relationships between these CIs to help understand service dependencies and improve root cause analysis during incidents. By tracking version changes and updates, we support better change control and risk management. Governance is maintained through strict access controls and audit trails, ensuring data confidentiality and integrity throughout the system lifecycle.

Contract & Warranty Tracking

Elitser's Contract and Warranty Tracking solutions offer centralized management of all IT-related agreements including software licenses, SLAs, AMCs, and vendor contracts. We implement automated alerts and reminders to notify stakeholders before renewal deadlines, warranty expirations, or contract terminations. Our services enable detailed tracking of vendor obligations and performance metrics, helping organizations enforce accountability and improve vendor relationships. With better visibility into contract data, organizations can make informed decisions, reduce risks, and optimize their procurement and budgeting strategies.
