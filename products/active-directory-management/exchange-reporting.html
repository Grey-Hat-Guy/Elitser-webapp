Track, audit, and report on every change made to mailbox permissions across your Exchange domains. Ensure strict access control, prevent unauthorized data exposure, and maintain a clear audit trail for compliance.

Audit all modifications to mailbox properties, including message size restrictions, storage quotas, and status changes like activation, deactivation, or moves. Stay informed about crucial configuration shifts and optimize resource allocation.

Get quick, reliable insights into your Exchange database health. Monitor database size, growth trends, storage restrictions, and structural changes to ensure optimal performance, plan capacity, and prevent downtime.
