What is Enterprise Service Management?

Extending IT service management capabilities beyond technology services to address business-centric use cases; managing service demand and supply through a common platform, portal, and service catalog; and speeding up innovation and workflow automation through PaaS/low-code development tooling.

- Charles Betz, Forrester

Why Do Organizations Need ESM?

To better contribute to business growth, employees need seamless services from IT, HR, Facilities, Legal, Travel, and other departments. While IT teams have mastered service delivery using proven ITSM best practices, most other departments still rely on outdated methods like emails, spreadsheets, or siloed legacy applications.

This fragmented approach, combined with the lack of established processes, makes efficient service delivery a significant challenge. Digital transformation has revolutionized customer experiences - it's time businesses extend the same level of consumer-focused service delivery to their internal teams.
